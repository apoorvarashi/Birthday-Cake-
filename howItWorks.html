How It Works

A simple guide to getting started with our rental platform

Finding Your Perfect Rental Home

Follow these simple steps to find and secure your ideal rental property

Step 1

Create Your Account

Sign up and complete your profile with verification to build trust with hosts.

Step 2

Search & Filter

Use our advanced filters to find properties that match your criteria - location, price, amenities, and more.

Step 3

Tour Virtually or In-Person

Schedule a viewing through our platform. Many properties offer virtual tours.

Step 4

Submit Your Application

Once you've found the perfect place, submit your rental application through our secure platform.

Step 5

Sign Lease & Move In

Review and sign your lease digitally, pay your security deposit, and prepare for move-in!

Renting Out Your Property

Follow these simple steps to list and manage your rental property

Step 1

Create Your Host Account

Sign up as a host and complete your profile verification to build trust with potential renters.

Step 2

List Your Property

Create a detailed listing with photos, description, amenities, and pricing information.

Step 3

Manage inquiries & Applications

Respond to inquiries, schedule viewings, and review rental applications through our platform.

Step 4

Screen Tenants & Sign Lease

Use our screening tools to verify tenant credentials and sign leases digitally.

Step 5

Receive Payments & Manage Your Rental

Get paid securely through our platform and use our tools to manage maintenance requests and communications.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my account?

Account verification requires a valid government ID and sometimes a proof of address. This helps ensure safety for all users.

What documents do I need to rent a property?

Typically, you'll need proof of income, identification, and rental history. Specific requirements vary by property.

How are payments processed?

All payments are processed securely through our platform. We never share your financial information with other parties.
